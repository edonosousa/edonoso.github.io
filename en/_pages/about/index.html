GrowNYC

Market Manager

2016 - 2017

GrowNYC is a non profit organization that aims to improve the lives and communities of New Yorkers through environmental programs and initiatives. I worked with the Greenmarket program as a Market Manager for the Poe Park and 175th Street Farmers Markets.

  • Managed daily operations for Farmers Market in the Bronx and Manhattan.
  • Connected New York City communities with their local farmers through outreach programs, educational activities and tabling events, to increase awareness of local, healthy and fresh food.
  • Communicated with customers in English and Spanish and translated marketing material and infographics to Spanish.

Chilean Navy

Coast Guard Officer

2004 - 2015

The Coast Guard of Chile is responsible for protecting the marine environment, natural resources of Chile, and lives at sea. My role as an Officer of the Coast Guard ranged from training and management of new officers to navigating a patrol boat through the Patagonia fjords to protect our natural resources.

  • Training and Development Manager for Chilean Navy Officers: Where I conducted and supervised training and development programs for 120 Midshipmen. Together with other Managers. That year include leading training programs aboard the Sail Training Ship “Esmeralda” in the Tall Ship Race “Velas Latinoamerica 2014”. Including classes, simulations, conferences and workshops.
  • Commander of a Coast Guard Patrol Boat: Overseeing the complete operation of a rescue patrol boat in the Patagonian fjords of southern Chile. While I lead a crew of 8 in various maritime activities, such as search and rescue, lighthouse maintenance and supporting local communities.
